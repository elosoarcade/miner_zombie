# Miner zombie
Miner Zombie is a Minecraft Bedrock add-on that adds a new class of zombie to underground biomes, making mining even more dangerous and challenging. ⛏️

Miner Zombies spawn between layers 40 and -16 below the surface, in groups of 2 to 4. They are slightly stronger and more resistant than a normal zombie. If they hit you, they apply the Mining Fatigue effect for 60 seconds. They can spawn holding anything from sticks to pickaxes of all material tiers, all of which drop when defeated, along with mining materials such as bronze ore, iron, emeralds, and diamonds—so fighting them can come with a solid reward.

❤️ Health: 30-40 | ⚔️ Attack: 3 | ✨ EXP: 7

This addon is optimized to run effectively on Realms and dedicated servers with minimal hardware resource consumption and its available in english and español. 🇬🇧 🇲🇽
